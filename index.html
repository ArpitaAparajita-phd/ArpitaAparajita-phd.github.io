About Our Lab

Our lab focuses on understanding the molecular mechanisms and therapeutic strategies for colorectal cancer and oral cancer. We aim to identify novel biomarkers, study tumor progression, and develop innovative treatment approaches, including targeted therapies and immunotherapies. Through cutting-edge research, we strive to contribute to cancer diagnosis, treatment, and improving patient outcomes.
